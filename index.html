Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about life insurance

How much life insurance do I need?

+

A general rule of thumb is 10-12 times your annual income. However, your specific needs depend on factors like outstanding debts, number of dependents, and future expenses like college tuition. Our agents can help you calculate the right amount.

Do I need a medical exam?

+

Not always. Many of our carriers offer no-exam policies for coverage up to $500,000. These policies typically have instant or same-day approval. For larger coverage amounts, a simple health questionnaire or medical exam may be required.

What's the difference between term and whole life?

+

Term life insurance covers you for a specific period (10, 20, or 30 years) and is typically more affordable. Whole life insurance covers you for your entire life and builds cash value, but costs more. We'll help you choose based on your goals and budget.

How long does it take to get approved?

+

For no-exam policies, approval can happen within minutes to 24 hours. For policies requiring a medical exam, the process typically takes 2-4 weeks from application to approval.

Will my quote cost me anything?

+

No. Getting a quote is 100% free with no obligation. We're paid by the insurance carriers, not by you, so our service costs you nothing.
